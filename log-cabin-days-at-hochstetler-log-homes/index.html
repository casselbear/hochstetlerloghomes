2024 Log Cabin Days

$5 admission fee for adults – children 12 & under free.

Friday, September 13th - 10AM to 6PM

Saturday, September 14th, 9AM to 5PM

(Out of respect to our tour hosts, please don't stop at any home after 5:30PM on Friday or 4:30PM on Saturday)

Suggested donations are $25 per person. All proceeds will benefit the American Cancer Society and Mohican Parochial School.

You will have the rare opportunity of touring furnished log homes all within 1 hour of Hochstetler Log Homes. You will need to register for the car tour and pick up your map at the information booth when you arrive at Log Cabin Days.

Check out the HOMES that will be featured on our 2024 tour.
