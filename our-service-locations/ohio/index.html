Testimonials & Information

After buying 17 acres of rolling countryside south of Canton, Ohio in 1984, our dream of owning a log home finally became a reality in 2007. Our builder suggested we buy our logs from Hochstetler Milling, an Amish-owned company whose log homes he'd been building since 1999. I worked with Hochstetler's in-house designer to crate a plan that would suit our lifestyle, while Clayton worked outside, garding and contouring the site himself. We took our time throughout the process and the finished product is exactly what we envisioned... with quality throughout.


-Susan Livengood, Ohio

Carrie and I researched log homes for 3 years before finally reaching our decision. While walking through several log home shows and talking to suppliers, one comapny stood out from the rest - Hochstetler Milling. And, after envisioning how the Pleasant Hill model would offer an outstanding view of the scenic landscape, especially from the full-length rear deck... we were sold.
